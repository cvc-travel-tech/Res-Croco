What is a group leader?
The common definition of a group leader is a person who works with the staff of a group tour company,
in which he is selling the tour for a group of people in exchange for free travel and other benefits.
Additionally, a group leader can easily be anyone involved with a church, social club, sports team, religious group,
or an organization that enjoys travelling. He might also be someone with a group of family or friends who like traveling together.
So, if you are fond of taking a leadership role in a group, organizing tours and acting as the main contact person, we assure you a free travel!
