Who are we?

Cadence Vacations Center is one of the few DMCs in Egypt (Destination Management Companies). We maintain in-depth local knowledge of the area and we provide professional services. CVC is a Joint-stock company with a total capital of 10,000,000 EGP. We are proud to be licensed under the Egyptian Tourism Authority; license number "142214".

Why Cadence Vacations Center

Aiming at delivering the highest quality tours, we partner with best companies in the field of tourism such as: well-known hotel chains, luxuries Nile cruises, finest transportation enterprises and more.

We are devoted to making sure your trip is seamless by taking care of every detail of your vacation. Our experts know how to best value your time and money!

Variety of tours in Egypt is offered to meet your expectations. Whether you have only read about your destination or you are in deep discovery, we guarantee you will find the right tour

Our holiday grants you a chance to experience not only the popular, but also the little-known touristic attractions in the Land of Pharos.

We provide Wi-Fi device throughout the trip allowing you to share your best moments with your family and friends back home.

Finally, we promise a VIP treatment for each and every individual of our customers. Unrivalled advice and support will be present every single day of your trip. In addition, you will be granted private guided tours system all over the journey even within your Nile cruise excursions.
